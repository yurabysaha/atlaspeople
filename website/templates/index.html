Hardware development

Hardware design covers the conversion of hardware functional concepts into technical specifications and customer requirement documents; conversion specifications and requirements into block diagrams, circuit schematics, physical printed circuit boards layout.

Prototyping and debugging

Unlike software development, hardware development requires hardware. That is why we need to make prototypes for testing. At this stage, we can check the functionality of the future product and make changes if necessary.

Software and firmware development

Most electronic devices include a microprocessor that controls the operation of the device, processes external signals, etc. Also, it is often necessary to establish interaction between the user and/or other systems (server, database, gateway, etc).

Product finalization

We offer services to find partners for manufacturing. The final product consists not only of components but of the processes of its production.
